L'Ardigeur

About

At L'Ardigeur we seek to refine daily rituals. Superb coffee, from fair trade locations across the globe. Pens and Inks from your favourite and most trusted brands. Journals and notebooks to last. And afterhours we relax with scotches, whiskeys, bourbons, and charcuterie from local butchers and artisans.
We'll see you in soon.

- Coffee -

Our coffees are sourced from fair trade organic farms across the globe. with a wide selection, you're guaranteed to find your fit whatever your type or taste.

What's your preferred method? French Press, Percolator, AeroPress, Drip? Whether it's take-away or take-to-your-table, we've got you covered.

- Pens & Inks -

A small in-house collection of Western and Japanese nibs and pens from your favourite brands including Faber-Castell, Lamy, Parker, Pelikan, Sailor, TWSBI, and Pilot.

We have a love for inks, whether large run or small private collections. We maintain a selection from one of our favourite small batch producers, Robert Oster (our personal favourite is "Knight to Bishop") as well as trusted Pelikan and Faber-Castell.

- Journals & Notebooks -

Journaling - a practice that dates back centuries and contributes to idea generation and mental health. Our in-house favourites, including Mind Journal and The Subtle Art of Not Giving A F*ck Journal, are always stocked, as well as several open-ended versions.

Drawing, doodling, daily tasks, etc. Whatever it might be, we've got you covered with beautiful notebooks for every day use: Moleskine, Leuchtturm1917, Rhodia, Karst Stone, and Apica.

Afterhours

When the sun goes down, L'Ardigeur settles into a boutique lounge for late night drinks and charcuterie based on local butchers and artisans. Thursday through Saturday.
